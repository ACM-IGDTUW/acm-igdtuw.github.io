Acing the Smart India Hackathon

Smart India Hackathon is a nationwide initiative to provide students with a platform to solve some of the daily life problems and to inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem-solving. Under the Computer Science Education Week 2020, ACM IGDTUW organized a one-hour session on learning the tricks and tips to ace the Smart India Hackathon on 12th Dec 2020 from 6-7 PM. Speakers of the session were winners of SIH 2020: Komal Kirti, Pallavi Bishnoi, Akanksha Chandna, Lubhani Agarwal, Kuja, and Yashi Madan. The event received enthusiastic student participation. Certificates were provided to all the participants.
