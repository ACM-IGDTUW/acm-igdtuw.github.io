CHURN RISK RATE PREDICTION

The term "churn prediction" refers to identifying which customers are most likely to stop using a service or to end their subscription. In this work, at first data preprocessing and then classification is performed by comparing the Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and Decision Tree. Finally we evaluate the work using precision, recall (sensitivity),accuracy and F1-score. Based on the analysis of the result, Random Forest performs the best and results the highest accuracy of 91% and F1 score of 0.87.
