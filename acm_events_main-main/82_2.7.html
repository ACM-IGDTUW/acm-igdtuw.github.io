TWITTER ANALYSIS OF INDIAN UNICORNS AND TOP COMPANIES OF THE WORLD

In this research project, the authors have compared the four top companies of the world on the basis of their market value with the four Indian unicorns. The authors have used various factors like follower count, frequency of tweeting, retweeting and replies and date and time of posting to see how the pattern of followers varies for various companies based on their activeness, time zone of posting and types of posts they post. This paper provides analysis on the data of one week. The analysis is made by scraping twitter data from twitter API and using python data analysis and visualization techniques.
