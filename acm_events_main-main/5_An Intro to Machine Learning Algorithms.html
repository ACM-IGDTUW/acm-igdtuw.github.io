An Intro to Machine Learning Algorithms

ACM IGDTUW presented the Hour Of Code as a celebration of Computer science education week from 7th Dec to 13 Dec. Session was conducted on 10th December 2020, by Astika Nehra, an alumna of IGDTUW, an incoming SWE intern at Oracle, former Microsoft Learn Ambassador (Beta), Research and development Intern at TCS iON, Intern at Cisco ThingQbator and Summer Intern at CDAC. Topics like Applications of ML, Classification algorithms, Regression algorithms, Clustering algorithms (K-means) Deep fake AI, were covered in the session. It was a fun learning session and the response of the audience was eminent.
